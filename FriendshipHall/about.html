    Friendship Hall SF Retaurant

Built in 1959, the Friendship Banquet Hall has been providing the city of San Jose a venue for all occasions. In 2010 the Friendship Banquet Hall began a new phase with continued enhancements and has become a full service event site.
Whether it be a casual employee luncheon, sophisticated cocktail party, an elegant wedding and even a Quinceanera our knowledgeable staff will be there to offer support and advice from start to finish.



Our menu is designed around all occasions and our Executive Chef will be available
for any questions you may have while working on creating an unforgettable dinning experience.

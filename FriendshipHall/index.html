Established

We have rich history in the heart of Bay Area. We became established in 1959. Friendship Banquet Hall is located in San Jose, CA. Our venue is mainly used for weddings, quincenieras and corporate events.

Our Crew

We have an excellent in-house catering team that serves a variety of delectable preparations offering services like event planning and transportation. We have customized packages targeting private events.
